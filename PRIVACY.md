# 隐私说明 / Privacy

## 中文

BaziChart 是本地运行的 macOS 应用。

- 不要求注册或登录。
- 不包含广告、分析 SDK、崩溃上报或用户追踪。
- 排盘计算和行政区划搜索均在设备上完成。
- 姓名、出生时间、地区、经度和排盘结果不会由应用上传。
- 用户主动保存的命例存储在当前 macOS 用户的 `UserDefaults` 中，删除 App 不一定自动删除这些偏好数据。
- “复制完整排盘”会将结果写入系统剪贴板。其他有剪贴板权限的应用可能读取该内容。

发布 Issue、截图或诊断信息前，请删除真实姓名、出生日期、地点等个人信息。

## English

BaziChart runs locally on macOS.

- No registration or sign-in is required.
- The app contains no advertising, analytics SDK, crash uploader or user tracking.
- Chart calculation and administrative-area search happen on-device.
- The app does not upload names, birth data, locations, longitude or chart results.
- Profiles saved by the user are stored in the current macOS user's `UserDefaults`. Removing the app may not remove those preferences automatically.
- “Copy full chart” writes the result to the system clipboard. Other apps with clipboard access may be able to read it.

Remove real names, dates of birth and locations before posting issues, screenshots or diagnostic information.
